ReGreen Works

We, at ReGreen works, try to reduce the amount of waste by using basic as well as complex Decomposition and Recycling
Processes, hence creating awareness among people for environmental care and also creating equal
employement opportunities for various craft work individuals.

Click here to Get Started!

Services We Offer

Recycling

Recycle various products by yourself!
If you are unable to, Our affiliated organisations will do it for you.

Decomposition

Decompose household products by using easy methods or getting in contact with organisations who will do it for you!

About Our Craft Work individuals

Get in contact with Small scale craft work individuals who will help you conduct your processes safely and also assist you in obtaining a better quality of Decomposed/Recycled product.
