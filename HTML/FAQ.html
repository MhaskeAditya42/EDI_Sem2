Frequently Asked Questions

How much waste is produced in India?
India generates 62 million tonnes of waste each year. About 43 million tonnes (70%) are collected of which about 12 million tonnes are treated and 31 million tonnes are dumped in landfill sites
Who is a Craft Work Individual?
A craft work individual is a person who practices handicraft as a job. As the pandemic hit, many workers have lost thier jobs and in this need of time, we provide an employement based on your responses which help them make a living.
What is the ideal response time from organisations?
As soon as your waste is delivered, the organisation will respond to you within 4 weeks and your recycled product will be delivered to you accordingly.
What if the waste I have doesnt fit into any category?
If thats the case, you can try contacting our us from our CONTACT US page so we can guide you further.
